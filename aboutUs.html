Mission Statement

What is the mission of Sandra's Soldiers? Sandra created this organization with one mission in mind....

To serve others.

Sandra's Soldiers is on a mission to bring true mental health advocacy to collegiate athletics, from the athletes themselves!. Sandra decided the best way to help the college athletes in need of support, was to allow the student athletes (current or former), to lead this movement in a major way.

Sandra's Soldiers is on a mission to bring a culture of unity and care to the daily struggles of each student athlete. We acknowledge that the student athlete experience is a vast one, and deserves attention, advocacy and understanding from all perspectives.


Vision Statement

Our vision as an organization is to encourage and facilitate a safe space for collegiate athletes to have healthy conversations about their struggles, and help guide each other to a deeper understanding of themselves.
